Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for Pizza. It is a delicious dish that is sure to please everyone at your table. It is a great dish for any occasion, and it is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a pizza pan.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add the pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, green bell pepper, and red onion.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Enjoy!
