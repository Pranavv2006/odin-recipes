Momo

Description

This is a recipe for Momo. It is a delicious dish that is sure to please everyone at your table. It is a great dish for any occasion, and it is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • Minced chicken - 1 cup
  • Minced garlic - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced ginger - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced scallions - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced cilantro - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced mint - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced basil - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced parsley - 1 tablespoon
  • Minced oregano - 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the minced chicken, minced garlic, minced ginger, minced scallions, minced cilantro, minced mint, minced basil, minced parsley, and minced oregano.
  2. Form the mixture into small balls.
  3. Cook the balls in a pan with a little oil.
  4. Enjoy!
