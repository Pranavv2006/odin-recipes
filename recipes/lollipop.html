Apple Chicken Lollipop

Description

This is a recipe for Apple Chicken Lollipop. It is a delicious dish that is sure to please everyone at your table. It is a great dish for any occasion, and it is sure to impress your guests.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of Worcester sauce
  • 1/2 cup of rice vinegar
  • 1/2 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup of apple juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the butter, brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, Worcester sauce, rice vinegar, apple cider vinegar, and apple juice.
  3. Place the chicken lollipops in a baking dish and pour the sauce over them.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Enjoy!
